PIXEL

The youngest of the group, he is smilly and friendly.

He is 9 years old, he was born in Shanghai but he moved to Toulouse right after his birth. He is the leader of the group, he takes good decisions thanks to his high IQ. However, he is sometimes lazy and likes to eat bamboo.

His past

Shanghaï : 2015 - 2018

He was born in Shanghaï, but right after his birth, he was adopted by Mr. Jean-Marc Benito. He was raised and feed by him. After 3 years he was able to integrate the school of his adopted father, the IPI school where he became a student.

Toulouse : 2018 - 2019

He was a true product of informatic, he was a genius above everyone else. Besides his young age and the fact that he was a panda, his grades were to the top, his comrades were jealous of his intelligence and the teachers despised him because he already knew everything they were teaching. He was able to graduate within a year.

Toulouse : 2019 - Today

Mr. Jean-Marc Benito offers him to stay in the school and to become the mascot and leader of all students. Pixel accepted this offer and now resided in the IPI office, mostly known as the aquarium, helping students with their projects when they need him.

His projects

Panda : 2015 - 2016

During the first few weeks of his life, he wanted to be a lazy panda doing nothing but rolling and eating bamboo.

University director : 2016 - 2018

After his meeting with Mr. Jean-Marc Benito, he wanted to become like him, the director of a huge university. However, he prefered science over informatic so he wanted to become the director of the Sorbonne in Paris.

Computer engineer : 2018 - 2019

While studying at the IPI school, he wanted to become a great computer engineer, the freedom and creativity this work provided him was what he wanted the most. He wanted to go far away, work in a big company.

IPI mascot : 2019 - Today

He couldn't leave his school and adopted father. He really loved this school, the students, his father and wanted to work at the IPI. The job as a mascot Mr. Jean-Marc Benito offered to him was a revelation. He still works as such and loves his job.
